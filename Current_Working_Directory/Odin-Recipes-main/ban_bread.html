Delicious Banana Bread

A perfect way to use those overripe bananas!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (240g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup (113g) unsalted butter, softened
  • ¾ cup (150g) brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups (about 4-5) mashed overripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • ½ cup chopped nuts or chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  3. In a separate bowl, cream together the butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in the eggs, mashed bananas, and vanilla extract (if using) until well blended.
  4. Gradually stir the banana mixture into the flour mixture, mixing just until moistened. Fold in nuts or chocolate chips if using.
  5. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 60 to 65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean.
  7. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.

Tips for Perfect Banana Bread

  • Use very ripe bananas for the best flavor. The more brown spots, the better!
  • Don't overmix the batter. Stir just until the ingredients are combined.
  • For extra moisture, add ¼ cup of sour cream or Greek yogurt to the batter.
  • Customize your bread by adding different mix-ins like dried fruits, coconut, or spices like cinnamon or nutmeg.
